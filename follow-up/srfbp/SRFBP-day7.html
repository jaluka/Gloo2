Speed Reading For Business Professionals
7 Day Follow-Up Program

Day 7

Effective Reading
- Progress Check

Today is the final day of this short follow-up program and so it's time to do a quick test of your reading speed to see how much you have progressed over the last 7 days.

Firstly download and print the following Reading Test which is in an identical format and of the same level to the initial benchmark reading test you did at the workshop

Download Second Reading Test (opens in new browser window)

Now give yourself 1 minute to read as much of the article as you can. Then calculate your reading speed and add it to the 'your reading speed' handout which is in the 'progress' section of your manual.

Your score for this test should go in the space for 'second test'.

If you have done the RocketReader practice and the daily reading exercises in this follow-up program, then your reading speed should have improved significantly compared to the initial reading test you did. And the good news is that the more you practise the more you will continue to improve.

So now that you are familiar with how to practise your new reading skills, continue on doing at least 10 minutes a day practice on most days for the next week. Choose the exercises and activities which work best for you. At the end of this period you will have done 2 weeks of focused, regular practice. So then download and print the following Reading Test and complete a final test

Download Final Reading Test (opens in new browser window)

Again, add your score for this test to the 'your reading speed' handout in the space for 'final test'. Also now calculate the increase in your reading speed both in terms of words per minute and also as percentage improvement compared to your starting reading speed. This will show you how far you have progressed in a relatively short period of time - and with minimal effort and time commitment!

Final Thoughts

As you know, we tend to forget 80% of the information we take in within 24 hours. What's more, research suggests that up to 95% of all training is wasted because it is not remembered or simply not used.

But fortunately that's not the case for you. Because if you've completed the exercises contained in this short follow-up program then you've been actively applying what you learned at the workshop - and you've started to acquire some powerful new reading habits.

And best of all, you can now read faster - hopefully much faster! - than before.

Congratulations on completing this 7 Day Follow-Up Program!
