Speed Reading For Business Professionals
7 Day Follow-Up Program

Day 2

Today's Activities

The aim of today is to continue to lay the foundation for your effective reading habits by initially focusing on the core reading skills - namely your ability to take in information quickly (flash exercise) and building your comfort level with and habituation to reading with a guide.

Tomorrow we will start to look at reading faster.

RocketReader
- Flash Exercise

Spend a few minutes practising the 'flash' exercise. Relax your eyes and focus on capturing the words accurately as they flash up. Aim for 100% accuracy!

Reading Practice
- Using A Visual Guide

Now spend at least 5 minutes - longer if you can - reading your chosen book or material. As with yesterday, the aim at this stage is to simply start getting used to reading with a Visual Guide.

Memory Skills
- Review What You Know

At the workshop we covered the keys to memory. Take a moment now to mentally recall what we covered. Then open up your manual and refresh your memory by flicking through the 'memory' section handouts. This simple, quick activity will help reinforce the information while it is still fresh.

End of Day 2!
