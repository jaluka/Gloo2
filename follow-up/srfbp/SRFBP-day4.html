Speed Reading For Business Professionals
7 Day Follow-Up Program

Day 4

Today's Activities

The focus for today, like yesterday, is to start reading at a faster pace than that at which we are have been reading in the past. To break slow-reading habits! So let's continue.

RocketReader
- Speed Exercise

In the main menu select the 'speed' exercise. When prompted choose a text on which to practise. The program will then open up and you will see a box in which it will display your chosen text broken up in to chunks or groups of words, one group at a time. You will see there are some settings you can adjust, namely

  • speed - select the words per minute you'd like to read at

  • width - varies the chunk size or number of words displayed each time

  • ramp - allows you to progressively increase the speed as you read your text

Have a play with the settings and familiarise yourself with the exercise. Then select a comfortable level, focus and read your selected text as it flashes up in front of you. Each text excerpt only takes a few minutes to read from start to end.

If you are also able to spend a few minutes practising the 'Flash' exercise then this is also beneficial.

Reading Practice
- Increasing Speed

Similar to yesterday, spend at least 5 minutes in total reading your chosen book or material. Read using a guide. For the initial half of the time, focus on getting in to a good comfortable reading pace and rhythm. Then for the second half of the time, use your guide to deliberately force yourself to read faster so that you really need to focus 100%.

Remember - it should be a challenge but if you lose comprehension when you are reading then you're probably going a little too fast at this stage.

End of Day 4!
