Speed Reading For Business Professionals
7 Day Follow-Up Program

Day 1

Introduction

It's great that you've chosen to take this 7 Day Follow-up Program. It will provide you with a quick and easy way to practise and build on the key skills and techniques we discussed at the workshop plus provide you with a framework to do so.

If you do the minimum, it should take you just 10 minutes a day. If you want to do more if you have the time and are able to, that's also helpful. But as a minimum at least 10 minutes a day for the next 7 days in order to further increase your reading speed and start to establish some new and more effective reading habits.

I'd also recommend doing the daily activities at the same time each day so you're dedicating a specific time each day to practising your new skills and are therefore more likely to do them when planned.

You'll also want to choose some reading material on which to practise. Perhaps a book you've been meaning to read or possibly something work-related if that will be beneficial for you. So let's get started.

RocketReader
- Flash Exercise

If you haven't already done so, set up your access to RocketReader by signing up for a free 10 day trial at the following website

www.RocketReader.com (opens in new browser window)

Once you've spent a few minutes familiarising yourself with the program and settings spend a few minutes practising the 'flash' exercise. Choose a speed which is not too easy and not too hard for you but enough of a challenge that you really need to focus in order to see all the words as they flash up. Also choose a number of characters which is not too difficult initially. Most people find 12 characters achievable to start. You're all good to go now.

Reading Practice
- Using A Visual Guide

Today spend at least 5 minutes - longer if you can - reading your chosen book or material with the aim of simply getting used to reading with a Visual Guide.

Don't worry about pushing yourself to read at a faster pace at this stage. You'll focus on that later in the program. The initial aim is simply to get comfortable with reading using a guide.

End of Day 1!
