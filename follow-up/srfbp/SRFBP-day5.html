Speed Reading For Business Professionals
7 Day Follow-Up Program

Day 5

Today's Activities

The purpose of today's activities is again to push you to increase your reading speed a little further. Plus to begin to apply the ideal reading approach.

RocketReader
- Speed Exercise

As you did yesterday, open up the 'speed' exercise in RocketReader. This time adjust the settings to select a comfortable starting level, though this time set the 'ramp' function to 25%. This will increase the speed at which you read the text as the exercise progresses until when you finish you are reading at 25% faster than when you started. If you like, repeat the exercise with a different text and a higher starting speed - and if you are up for a challenge - a higher 'ramp' rate, perhaps 30% or even 50%! This will give you a feel for what it is like to read at very high speed.

Your Reading Approach
- Moving Towards Active Reading

Choose a chapter or a section of 10 to 20 pages from your book and read it as quickly as you can using a Visual Guide. When finished mentally ask yourself what were the key points? Then re-read the section at a slightly slower pace. Again, mentally review what you've just read and then imagine turning to a friend and giving them a 60 second summary of what it was all about.

This process of active reading and then summarising the information - even just mentally - is an ideal way to learn any information. So start applying this approach regularly to important information you need to learn and know.

End of Day 5!
