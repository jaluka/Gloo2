Memory Skills For Business Professionals
7 Day Follow-Up Program

Day 3

Key Memory Skills
- Visualisation and Linking

1) Visualise the following 10 words, picturing them as clearly and in as much detail as possible...

Laptop computer
Candlestick
Peace
Baseball bat
Referee
Procedure
Julia Gillard
Vegemite
Blue
Four wheel drive

2) Now find at least 2 different ways of linking the following pairs of words together, using your imagination, your senses and lots of action!

Blue - Referee
Candlestick - Julia Gillard
Laptop computer - Vegemite
Baseball bat - Procedure
Peace - 4 Wheel Drive

Memory Applications
- To Dos

At the workshop we went through the Number Rhyme technique. Take a moment to review your images for the numbers 1 to 10.

Now use the Number Rhyme to memorise the main things that you need to do today or tomorrow. As with the above visualisation and linking exercise use your imagination to create vivid - and memorable - connections which will easily remind you of the information.

End of Day 3!
