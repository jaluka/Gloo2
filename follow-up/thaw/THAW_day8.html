21 Day Follow-Up

Day 8

Memory

Memory Applications - Journeys

REVIEW - Yesterday you tested your power of visual association by memorising the first 20 elements of the periodic table using your House Journey.

Take a moment now and see how many of the 20 elements you still recall.

Memory Applications - New Journey

And now it's time to expand your storage capacity and create your second journey.

For this new journey, use a previous house you've lived in or perhaps a work location. Your journey should include at least 5 rooms or stages, each containing at least 4 stops or items - so 20 things in total.

Use the information mapping software you downloaded to brainstorm and build up your journey.

Once you've created your new journey, run through it in your mind several times during the course of the day to really fix it in your memory.

Memory Applications - Names

Think of an image for the following 5 female names beginning with 'B' and record them in your Visual Vocabulary file for female names. Add a location for each name if you find this helpful….

Becky
Belinda
Beth
Bianca
Brenda

When you've done this, close your eyes and picture your images for the 5 names once more.

End of Day 8!
