21 Day Follow-Up

Day 10

Memory

Memory Applications - Names

REVIEW - Yesterday you created visual reminders for 5 male names beginning with 'B'. What was your image for…

Brian?
Benny?
Bruce?
Bob?
Brad?

Open up your Visual Vocabulary files and review the visual reminders you created.

You've now created visual reminders for 20 first names, in addition to the names of the people you met at your workshop.

So by now you've had a good amount of practice at focusing on a name, engaging your imagination and creating reminders.

The next step is to start putting this into practice in real life - if you haven't already!

So from today onwards… pick 3 people you meet each day, create a visual reminder for their first name and link it to the person using lots of movement, action and imagination. And add any new first names to your Visual Vocabulary files.

If you don't meet 3 'new' people each day, then simply use people you already know. For example, work colleagues or suppliers who you know by sight but perhaps you don't know their name. Or you do know their name but you sometimes have trouble remembering it.

And make it a habit to spend a couple of minutes at the end of each day to mentally review who you met - and any new images you created for first names.

You'll be amazed how this simple action will help lock people's names into your long-term memory… and quickly build up your Visual Vocabulary.

That's it for today… pick 3 people, memorise their names and then review the images and connections you created at the end of the day.

End of Day 10!
