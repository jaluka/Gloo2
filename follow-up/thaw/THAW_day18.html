21 Day Follow-Up

Day 18

Memory

Memory Applications - Names

REVIEW - Who did you meet yesterday? Create images for any new first names and add them to your Visual Vocabulary files.

Pick 3 people you met yesterday - or who you already know - and turn their surnames into a visual reminder. Connect your images to the people and record them in your surnames file.

Key Memory Skills - Observation And Visual Recall

REVIEW - Yesterday you paid special attention to people's noses. Picture some of the people you met and try to recall what their noses looked like.

Today repeat the exercise, this time focusing on chins. Notice all the different shapes and sizes that you see.

Memory Applications - Journey Technique

Yesterday you explained the number rhyme technique to a friend, family member or colleague.

Today, have a go at explaining the journey technique to somebody. Show them how they might use the technique and answer any questions they may have.

Memory Applications - Journey To Work

And now it's time to create one more journey.

You'll find this really easy as we'll use your journey to work. Of if you work from home (or you're lucky enough not to work!) then use a journey you know well such as to a friend's house.

Start at your front door and then just simply imagine making the trip, noticing key buildings and landmarks along the way.

Aim for at least 20 stops - though if you can get 30 or more so much the better.

You may choose to use the mind mapping software to help build up your journey and decide on stops that work well for you.

Once you've created your new journey, run through it in your mind several times during the course of the day to really fix it in your memory.

We'll take it for a spin tomorrow!

End of Day 18!
