21 Day Follow-Up

Day 20

Follow-up Programme - REVIEW

After 20 days you've almost reached the end of this follow-up programme.

So congratulations… and no new stuff today.

Instead, today we'll focus on reviewing what you've done over the last 3 weeks so that you get the most benefit from the time that you've invested.

To do this, simply open up a blank map and capture your answers to the following 3 questions…

- What were some of the exercises you did during this follow-up?
- What were the key things that you learned by doing the exercises?
- How will you now use the skills and techniques that you've learned?

Spend just 5 to 10 minutes to do this quick review.

REVIEW - Names

Review your Visual Vocabulary files for male names, female names and surnames.

REVIEW - Journeys

Review your new journeys…

- Second house journey
- Desk journey
- Journey to work

REVIEW - Mapping

Take a quick look at some of the maps you created over the last 20 days, in particular your goals for the next 12 months.

Now's also a good time to pause and identify at least one situation where you - and the people you work with - could benefit from using mapping as a group e.g. team meetings, project meetings, brainstorming and planning sessions.

You've now completed this follow-up programme. Tomorrow we'll briefly discuss where to next.

End of Day 20!
