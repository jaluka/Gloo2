21 Day Follow-Up

Day 11

Memory

Memory Applications - Names

REVIEW - Yesterday's task was simply to pick 3 people you met that day, create a visual reminder for each first name and then link it to the person. Plus add any new first names to your Visual Vocabulary files.

So who did you meet yesterday?

Picture each person clearly in your mind's eye and recall the visual reminder, the prompt you created for the person's name and how you linked it to them.

This brief review will reinforce your image and help lock their name into your long-term memory.

Make it a habit to spend a couple of minutes at the end of each day to mentally review who you met that day - and to create visual reminders for at least 3 people and their first names. And remember to add any new first names to your Visual Vocabulary files.

Information Mapping

Today you'll use your mapping software to create a map of all the things you'd like to achieve in your life in the next 12 months.

Just use the GO process…

Step 1) Generate

Quickly dump down as many things as you can think of. Don't worry about the order. And don't be concerned with how you'll achieve them either, we'll come back to that later.

Simply brainstorm and capture your thoughts with the mapping software. Remember to cover all areas of your life including professional, social, health, family etc. - whatever is important to you.

Step 2) Organise

Once you've emptied your brain and you start to dry up, group your ideas under a number of headings that make sense to you. This may stimulate further thoughts… just add them in.

Once you've done this you'll have a 1 page mind map reflecting all the key things you'd like to achieve in the next 12 months. Review your map at the end of the day and add in any further thoughts that come to you.

Over the coming days you'll develop your map into an action plan - prioritising your goals and identifying the actions you need to take to achieve them.

But for now, simply review your map and reflect upon what's important to you.

End of Day 11!
