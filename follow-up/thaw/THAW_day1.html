21 Day Follow-Up

Day 1

Memory

Key Memory Skills - Visualisation and Linking

1) Visualise the following 6 words, picturing them as clearly and in as much detail as possible...

Photocopier
Banana
Umbrella
Dentist
Birthday cake
Donkey

2) Now find at least 3 different ways of linking each of the following pairs of words together. Engage your imagination, involve your senses and include lots of movement and action!

Photocopier - Dentist
Donkey - Banana
Birthday cake - Umbrella


Memory Applications - Names

1) Take a moment now to download the following 'Visual Vocabulary' files. You'll use these for recording the images - the visual reminders - that you create for people's first names. Each file contains a separate sheet for images you create for names beginning with each letter of the alphabet.

To download, right click the link and choose 'Save target as' to save it to your desired location.

Visual Vocabulary - first names FEMALE

Visual Vocabulary - first names MALE

TIP - you may want to print out the files, hole punch them and put them in a ring binder for ease of use and reference.

2) Think of an image for the first name of each of the other people who attended the workshop. Record the names and your chosen images on the appropriate pages. Add a location if you choose.

When done, close your eyes and mentally review the names and your chosen images.

End of Day 1!
