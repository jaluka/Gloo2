21 Day Follow-Up

Day 13

Memory

Key Memory Skills - Visualisation and Linking

Time to give your creativity a good workout…

1) Using your imagination, your senses and lots of action think of as many different ways as possible to link together the following 2 words. Aim for at least 10 ways, but if you can think of more… even better.

Octopus - Swiss Army Knife

2) Now read through the following 15 pairs of words - as quickly as possible - and as you go, think of just 1 connection between each pair of words…

Nun - Dynamite
Oil - Performance review
Traffic lights - Iron
Fireman - Newspaper
Steamroller - Kevin Rudd
Strawberry jam - Barbecue
Trivial Pursuit - Protest
Microphone - Pay rise
Banjo - Ayers Rock
Kung fu - Conference call
Sleepy - Pineapple
Argument - Apartment block
Blueprint - Unicycle
Helicopter - Project plan
Stilettos - Chairman

3) And now that you're warmed up and your creative juices are flowing, have a go at memorising the capital cities of the following 5 countries, even if you already know them.

Create a visual reminder for each word and then simply connect the two together like you've just been doing…

Belarus - Minsk
Bulgaria - Sofia
Congo - Brazzaville
Paraguay - Asuncion
Qatar - Doha

Phew, all done! We may just come back to this tomorrow so take a moment and mentally review how you connected the 5 countries and their capitals.

Memory Applications - Names

REVIEW - Who did you meet yesterday?

Information Mapping

Open up the map you created of the key things you'd like to achieve in the next 12 months. The next thing to do is to quickly brainstorm the actions that will help you to achieve these goals.

So simply add sub-branches listing all the steps, actions and mini-goals that will help you accomplish each of your main goals.

For now just brainstorm and capture, tomorrow you'll review and prioritise them.

End of Day 13!
