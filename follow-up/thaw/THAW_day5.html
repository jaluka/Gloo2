21 Day Follow-Up

Day 5

Memory

Memory Applications - Names

REVIEW - Yesterday you created visual reminders for 5 female names beginning with 'A". What was your image for…

Annie?
Alexis?
Annette?
Angela?
Amanda?

If you need to, open up your Visual Vocabulary files and review the visual reminders you chose.

Now think of an image for the following 5 male names beginning with 'A'…

Aaron
Alan
Albert
Andrew
Arnold

Record them in your Visual Vocabulary file for male names. Add a location for each name if you find this helpful.

When you've done this, close your eyes and picture your images for the 5 names once more.

Memory Applications - Journeys

REVIEW - At the workshop we went through the journey technique and you created a 20 stop journey around your house.

Take a moment now and run through your house journey from start to finish as quickly as possible.

During the course of today run through your house journey several more times, each time trying to do it faster than the previous.

Think of at least 1 way you could use your house journey in the next 7 days.

End of Day 5!
