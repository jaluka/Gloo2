Booking Your Place Is Quick And Easy

You can book your place in just a few moments by clicking on the 'Register' button below. You'll then be taken to our secure shopping cart where you'll let us know your details and then complete your payment quickly and securely via PayPal or credit card.

If you are booking more than 1 place at a workshop simply change the quantity in the shopping cart to the number of places you would like to book - you will automatically receive a 10% discount off the total cost when you book 2 or more places at a workshop.

If you'd prefer to pay by bank transfer, you can also make your booking using our Workshop Booking Form (PDF form, opens in a new window). Simply complete and then fax or email back to us using the details on the form.
