Speed Reading For Business Professionals
In-House Half Day Training Course


60 Second Summary

Blast through emails, books and reports, save valuable time, be more productive. The benefits of speed reading are obvious. In this fun, fast-paced, speed reading training course you and your colleagues will discover the reading techniques used by highly effective people to read faster, boost their productivity and get more done each day. Plus go beyond traditional speed reading courses by learning how to remember much more of what you read through the use of powerful memory techniques.

Special - Save $500.
Details below.


Purpose Of Workshop

  • To give you a rapid and effective introduction to Speed Reading

  • To show you how to boost your reading speed, increase your recall
    and save precious time every business day

  • To provide you with a simple framework to practise your new skills

What You'll Learn

  • Speed Reading - how well do you currently read and what's possible?

  • Why most people read below their potential and with poor comprehension

  • 4 key techniques to increase your reading speed, focus and comprehension

  • How to quickly double your reading speed through effective practice

  • How to remember more of what you read using the BDA approach

  • Reading strategies for different materials and purposes

  • How to combine speed reading with other techniques to overcome information overload
