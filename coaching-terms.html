 Terms Of Engagement And Booking Conditions

A small number of conditions apply when you book your Gloo Personal Coaching Program or Coaching Session(s) and these are detailed below. When you pay your Invoice you are confirming that you are happy with and accept these conditions. So please take a moment to read them now, thanks.

Confirming Your Coaching

We have tentatively booked in for you the date(s) we have identified for your Coaching Program or Coaching Session(s) and we will hold the agreed date(s) for you for the next 3 business days while you arrange to pay your Invoice.

Changes To Booked Coaching Programs And Sessions

By paying your Invoice you are making a firm booking for the agreed Coaching Program or Coaching Session(s) on the agreed date(s) and at the agreed price. In the event of cancellation coaching fees are non-refundable.

However, if you do need to reschedule a particular session we will happily do so as long as you give us at least 7 days notice and we are able to identify a mutually convenient alternative date. If you wish to cancel or change a Coaching Program or Session you have booked please email us by clicking here

Changes To The Agreed Scope Of Work

The cost we have quoted you for the coaching we have agreed to deliver is based upon our understanding of your requirements at the time of engagement and as confirmed to you via email.

If you wish to make changes to the nature or scope of the agreed work we are happy to consider such requests. If such requests will result in additional preparation, travel or delivery time and/or costs we will advise you of these additional costs and ask you to accept them before agreeing to vary the original scope.
