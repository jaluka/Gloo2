Other Payment Options

If you prefer not to book online or you don't have a credit card or PayPal account - not a problem. You can also book and pay for your Gloo workshop in one of the following ways.

1) Book Via Email - Pay By Bank Transfer

If you'd like to pay via bank transfer simply complete the Workshop Booking Form then make your payment using our account details shown on the form.

Once your payment has been received we will email you your official Booking Confirmation including a receipted Tax Invoice for your payment plus further details about the workshop venue and timings.

2) Book Via Email - Pay By Cheque Or Money Order

If you'd like to pay via cheque or money order simply complete the Workshop Booking Form then make your cheque or money order payable to
