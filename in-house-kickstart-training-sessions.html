Rapid Kickstart Sessions
Introduction To Memory Skills/Speed Reading


If you're looking to run an interesting and valuable Lunch and Learn session, or if time is limited, then our Rapid Kickstart Training Sessions are an ideal solution. These fun, fast-paced workshops give you and your colleagues the opportunity to receive a rapid introduction to a single topic. So choose from one of the following 'off-the-shelf' Kickstart Sessions...

  • Introduction To Memory Skills

  • Introduction To Speed Reading

Or if you've got something specific you're looking to achieve or you'd like to cover, simply let us know and we can design a session to meet your specific needs.

Delivery Format

  • x1 session delivered at your offices or chosen venue

  • Session length tailored to the time you have available

  • Can be delivered to smaller or larger groups

Presenter

  • Sessions designed and delivered by Australian Memory Record Breaker Chris Lyons. An experienced trainer and facilitator, Chris also has over 10 years' commercial experience gained in senior management roles in large Australian and UK companies before starting to design and deliver Gloo Training Programs in 2004
